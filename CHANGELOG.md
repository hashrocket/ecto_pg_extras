## v0.1.1

## v0.1.0

Initial release.

Make a variety of Postgres functions available to the Ecto DSL so that you
don't have to.

- `coalesce/1` and `coalesce/2`
- `nullif/1`
- `greatest/1` and `greatest/2`
- `least/1` and `least/2`
- `lower/1`
- `upper/1`
- `between/3`
- `not_between/3`
